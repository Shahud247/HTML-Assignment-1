Biryani:

We serve single and double biryani servings with Raita and Salad. Our one plate is enough to fill your appetite but if you want more, you can also order double plates from us. We serve the finest and freshly prepared biryani in Karachi.




Zarda:

Done with biryani and looking for a sweet meal to overcome the spice level? We offer our traditional Zarda as well. Filled with almonds, mewa, it's the go-to dish for Meetha lovers who are looking for a post meal after Biryani.




Kheer:

So, if you don't like Zarda and prefer Kheer instead, we have it as well. We serve Kheer in Mitti Kay Bartan to double its taste and give you the ultimate sweet meal you have been looking for in Pakistan. Isn't that amazing?
